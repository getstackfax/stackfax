# Stackfax Executive Audit

The Stackfax Executive Audit is the high-trust review lane for teams using or planning Ai agents, automations, model routing, RAG, workflow tools, local hardware, cloud tools, or internal Ai systems.

The audit answers the questions executives, operators, and technical leaders need answered before scaling an Ai stack.

## Core Questions

Can this work?

Is it safe?

What will it cost?

Where is it wasting tokens or time?

What should we fix first?

Can we prove what happened if something breaks?

That is Stackfax.

---

## What It Sells

The Executive Audit does not sell Ai hype.

It sells:

- clarity
- risk reduction
- cost control
- workflow sanity
- audit readiness
- next-step priority
- confidence before scaling

---

## Package Shape

A Stackfax Executive Audit may include:

- current stack map
- workflow risk review
- token and cost waste review
- data access review
- approval-gate review
- logs and receipts review
- failure-mode review
- top 10 risks
- top 10 fixes
- 30-day action plan
- executive verdict

---

## Buyer Fit

Best fit for teams asking:

- Are we using the right models for the right jobs?
- Are we wasting money on premium-model calls?
- Are our agents allowed to touch too much?
- Are approval gates missing?
- Can we prove what happened in a run?
- Are we ready to let this touch customers, money, credentials, inventory, files, or production systems?
- What should stay manual?
- What should we fix before scaling?

---

## Executive Verdict Framing

The CEO package is not:

“What model should I use?”

It is:

“What can this system safely do, what is it costing us, and what breaks first?”
