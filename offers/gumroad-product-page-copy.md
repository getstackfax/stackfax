# Stackfax Gumroad Product Page Copy

This file contains draft copy for a future Gumroad product page for the Stackfax Quick Report.

The goal is to create a simple first-dollar product page without building a full app or checkout system.

## Product Name

Stackfax Quick Report

## Short Description

Get a manual report card for your Ai stack before you buy hardware, burn premium-model tokens, pay for overlapping subscriptions, or copy the wrong setup.

## Price

Starting early price:

$19

## Product Summary

Stackfax checks whether your tools, models, subscriptions, hardware, workflow, and permissions match the task.

You send your current stack, use case, and concerns.

Stackfax sends back a manual report with a rating, risk flags, verdict, and best next move.

## Best For

This is best for people asking:

- Do I need a Mac mini?
- Am I about to burn tokens?
- Is my OpenClaw setup overkill?
- Which Ai subscriptions do I actually need?
- Am I copying the wrong creator or vendor stack?
- What should require human approval?
- What is my safest next move?

## What You Get

A Stackfax Quick Report may include:

- Overall rating
- Stack Score
- Main verdict
- Risk flags
- Relevant badges
- Hardware verdict
- Token burn review
- Model subscription fit
- Approval gate warnings
- Best next move
- Suggested upgrade path
- 30-day recheck notes

## Supported Report Types

Current supported Quick Report types:

- Mac Mini Hardware Verdict
- Token Burn Audit
- OpenClaw Starter Stack Check
- Model Subscription Fit Check
- Business Automation Safety Check
- Vendor Stack Verdict
- General Stackfax Quick Report

## How It Works

1. Buy the Stackfax Quick Report.
2. Complete the intake form.
3. Stackfax reviews your stack manually.
4. Stackfax prepares your report.
5. Your report is delivered by email or approved delivery method.

## Delivery Window

Expected delivery target:

24 to 48 hours after intake is received and accepted.

Delivery may take longer if:

- Your intake is incomplete
- Your stack is unclear
- Follow-up questions are needed
- The request is outside the current Stackfax scope
- Unsafe private information needs to be removed

## Important Safety Rule

Do not submit:

- Passwords
- API keys
- Recovery codes
- Payment details
- Wallet information
- Private customer data
- Private legal documents
- Private financial account information
- Sensitive personal information

Stackfax only needs a description of your stack, workflow, tools, and concerns.

## What Stackfax Does Not Provide

Stackfax does not provide:

- Legal advice
- Financial advice
- Trading advice
- Security certification
- Compliance certification
- Guaranteed cost savings
- Guaranteed business outcomes
- Guaranteed automation safety
- Guaranteed model performance
- Done-for-you implementation
- Emergency support

## Early Manual Status

Stackfax Quick Reports are early and manual.

This means:

- A human reviews the intake
- A human prepares the report
- Report structure may improve over time
- Feedback may improve future templates
- Reports are guidance, not guarantees

## Early Buyer Bonus

Early paid Quick Reports may include a beta preview of the upcoming Stackfax Pro Report format.

This may show:

- What would improve the score
- What may break at scale
- What subscriptions may overlap
- What should not be automated yet
- What would justify local hardware
- What to recheck in 30 days

This is a beta preview, not a finished Pro product.

## Refund Expectation

If Stackfax cannot deliver your report, you should receive a refund.

If your intake is missing key details, Stackfax may ask follow-up questions before starting.

Because reports are manual and custom, delivered reports are generally reviewed case by case for refund requests.
