## Reddit-Safe Version

Use only when relevant and allowed.

The useful first step is usually to check whether the stack fits the job before adding more tools, models, subscriptions, agents, or hardware.

For a stack review, I would want to know:

- What workflow is this for?
- What tools/models are already involved?
- What is too slow, too expensive, or confusing?
- What can agents touch?
- What requires human approval?
- What would prove the setup is working?

I would not buy more hardware or subscriptions until those answers are clear.
